About Me

Working with front and back end development including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery, PHP, MySQL, React, Git/GitHub, Node.JS, Bootstrap, and Firebase. currently seeking opportunities to bring my passion and dedication to web development to your company.

Over the last six years I have been part of an innovative start-up company in the Solar Industry that was recently acquired by Solar.com
My key role as a Product Manager was being responsible for Front-End UI/UX Design, Marking Content, Ad Campaigns, Data Analytics,Customer Service and Graphic Design. It was in this role that I found a passion for web development.

After short time as a successful Sales Engineer I never gave up web development and decided to enroll at LearningFuze, an accelerated Web Development Program. I had the privilege to work on a variety of solo and group projects as both front-end and back-end roles.

I am predominantly a front-end developer and have experience in JavaScript, jQuery, PHP and Node.js with React. I am experienced in Git version control with both GitHub and Bitbucket as well as deployment operations.

My background in Design, Engineering, Management and Sales coupled with the knowledge I have gained as a web developer make me a great asset to any team. I am seeking my next opportunity to bring my passion for programming and strong work ethic to your company.
